BECKY KOLSRUD

"I've been a busy woman"

Becky Kolsrud (b.1984) lives and works in Los Angeles. She received her MFA from UCLA in 2012. Her work has recently been included in group exhibitions at Foxy Production and Maccarone gallery, both in New York.
