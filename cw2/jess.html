JESS SWAN

Known for her vibrant, colourful acrylic

Jessica Swan is a self taught abstract artist based in Perth, Western Australia where she lives with her husband and four children. Known for her vibrant, colourful acrylic and mixed media paintings created in her purpose built studio in the heart of her home.

THEIR WORK

Find out more about their work by looking into their individual pieces.

Cosmos

This new expressive collection of original mixed media artworks is colourfully complex with metallic gold highlights and delightful texture changes to keep the eye wandering.

Dreamer

"Paper Petals" collection Like Forever Blooms but for your walls! This small collection of "Paper Petals" originals speaks or femininty, playfulness and wonder. Everlasting beauty captured in an ethereal abstract composition.

Falling

Everlasting beauty captured in an ethereal abstract composition. Like Jess Swan's muse, the everlasting flower, there is an abundance of joy in every moment of each artworks existence.

Flutter

The textured composition along with its delicate gold details, almost botanical elements & subtle colour palette speaks of femininity, playfulness and wonder.
