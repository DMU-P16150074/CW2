SOPHIE TEA

"Breaking the traditional gallery route"

Sophie prides herself on breaking the traditional gallery route having made her name independently on social media.
After graduating with a first-class degree in Business, Sophie went on to start her entrepreneurial journey by developing a ‘Tinder for Art’ app. She began painting to fund this venture but soon found it more lucrative to pursue Sophie Tea Art.
Fast forward to now Sophie has studios, galleries and teams in both London and Sydney and a relentless demand for her artworks.
