MAISEY CLARK

"I'm so excited for the future"

Maisey Clark created her account MaiseyDraws in the beginning of lockdown. So far she'd gained just over 1000 followers and has a new venture of comissions in shoe design. Speaking to Maisey she said she's loving what she's doing and so happy that she took the risk to make the account. Maisey can't wait to see where her account will go.
